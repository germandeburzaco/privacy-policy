Privacy Policy – Crafter App

Last updated: November 2025

Crafter App respects your privacy. This document explains what information the application collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

Crafter App does not collect or store any personal information on external servers. All data you create or modify inside the app (projects, materials, timers, configuration settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device.

The app does not transmit, share, or upload your data to any online service.

2. Permissions Used

The application may request the following permissions depending on features you use:

  • Storage Access – to save and load your project files locally.
  • Internet Access – required only to display optional ads (if enabled). No user data is transmitted.

No permission is used to gather personal information.

3. Local Data Storage

All information is stored directly on your device, inside the app’s private storage. You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage from system settings.

4. Analytics and Advertising

Crafter App may use advertising networks (such as Google AdMob) to display optional ads. These networks may collect anonymized technical information such as device type or approximate location for ad targeting.

Crafter App itself does not collect, store, or access this information.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not directly interact with external services, except optional advertisement providers. You may consult their respective privacy policies for more details.

6. Children’s Privacy

The application does not collect personal information from children. It is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. The updated version will replace previous ones and will be available on the same URL.
